No "right" or "wrong" answers.

Aim: practice arguments for essays and get thinking!

Why do we make HTTP requests and receive responses over TCP/IP?

In groups, discuss this question and devise a 3 minute presentation giving an answer to this question from one of the following perspectives. You have ten minutes to prepare, and may produce visual aids.

  • Historical - the chain of events that lead to this situation. Which events were important and why?
  • Social - power and society. Who controls this situation? Why does it exist? Do we challenge it and if so how?
  • Economic - money and advantage. Who gains from us making and sending HTTP requests in this way?
